Who Are We?

Welcome to Event Announcements, your go-to platform for discovering exciting events in Ankara! We are a team of four students from Bilkent University's CTIS department, passionate about connecting people with diverse and engaging experiences in the city.


Our mission is to provide a comprehensive and user-friendly platform that showcases a wide range of events, including concerts, exhibitions, workshops, conferences, and much more.


Through our website, you can easily explore upcoming events, get detailed information about each event, and conveniently purchase tickets. We strive to ensure that every user has a seamless and enjoyable experience while discovering and attending events in Ankara.


Stay connected with us to stay informed about the latest event updates, exciting promotions, and exclusive offers by subscribing to our newsletter. Follow us on social media to join a thriving community of event enthusiasts and share your experiences.


We are committed to enhancing the event culture in Ankara and making your event-going experience unforgettable. Join us on this journey of exploration, inspiration, and celebration!
